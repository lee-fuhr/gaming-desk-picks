How we choose our picks

We base our picks on long-term owner feedback from forums like Reddit and Discords, published reviews from reputable tech outlets, and the reputation of each brand in the gaming and ergonomic communities. We don’t run our own timed trials or lab tests; instead we sift through hundreds of owner experiences to identify what holds up well over years of daily use. What actually breaks? Wobbly legs under heavy dual-monitor setups, cable-tray rails that strip after a few adjustments, and motor noise in budget height-adjustable desks.
